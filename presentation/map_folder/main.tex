            \begin{posterbox}{Goal: A Universal Investment Engine}
                To develop a scalable pipeline that automates the discovery of Real Estate Arbitrage opportunities by bridging disconnected data sources (Long-term Buy vs. Short-term Rent).
                \vspace{0.5cm}
                
                \textbf{The Global Problem:} 
                The market for buying homes and the market for Airbnb operate in silos. 
                \begin{itemize}
                    \item Investors lack a unified view of \textbf{Acquisition Cost vs. Potential Revenue}.
                    \item Manual analysis relies on "gut feeling" and misses high-yield pockets.
                \end{itemize}
            \end{posterbox}

            \begin{posterbox}{Methodology}
                We built a 4-stage automated pipeline focused on \textbf{Net Yield} calculation.
                \begin{enumerate}
                    \item \textbf{Ingestion:} Dynamic scraping of real estate listings (handling JS/Rotation).
                    \item \textbf{Feature Engineering:} Extracting "inclusive" comparison features (Luxury flags, Penthouse status, exact bed/bath counts).
                    \item \textbf{Geospatial Mapping:} Exact lat/lon matching using ArcGIS to link sales to rental zones.
                    \item \textbf{Predictive Modeling:} Random Forest Regressor trained on Airbnb ground truth to forecast revenue.
                \end{enumerate}
            \end{posterbox}

            \begin{posterbox}{Case Study Implementation: NYC}
                We deployed the engine on \textbf{New York City} due to its high data complexity and strict anti-bot protections.
                
                \vspace{0.5cm}
                \textbf{Data Sources:}
                \begin{itemize}
                    \item \textbf{Buy Side:} Scraped \textit{Properstar.com} (Real-time Listings).
                    \item \textbf{Rent Side:} Official \textit{InsideAirbnb} dataset.
                    \item \textbf{Volume:} Trained on \textbf{24,437} verified Airbnb listings (Ground Truth) to ensure statistical significance.
                \end{itemize}
            \end{posterbox}
